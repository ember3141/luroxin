Elanore Rigby for Violin, Cello, and Piano

Date: 2025   Status: Completed

I wrote these parts to be performed with my friends. One of them is a talented cellist, so I have assigned him the full melody. However, my other friend does not know how to play the piano outside of a few simple block chords. The piano part is greatly simplified for this reason. I balanced the melody between the violin and cello parts in order to make it interesting.

After a quick search online, it is safe to say I have made the most complete version of a Elanore Rigby transcription for violin, cello, and piano.

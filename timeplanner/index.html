Visual Time Planner Webapp

Date: 2022   Status: Completed

I have searched long and hard for some kinda planner tool that allows you to block off your time in a particular way.
Each time I come home from school or work, I am given a chunk of time I must ration. However, with how many activities I have, it is difficult to simply have an hourly schedule to follow. Who knows how long dinner may take, or if something unexpected comes up. Hourly planners do not account for the realistic flexibility I need.
Out of frustration I made my own program to do this. Check it out at at the link below.

https://ember3141.github.io/time-planner/
